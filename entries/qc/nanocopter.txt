Some really small quadrocopters

It's always fascinating how far you can minaturize nowadays. 
A selection of really small quadrocopters (UAV, VTOL, Mikrocopter):
<ul>
<li><a href="http://aero.stanford.edu/mesicopter/">
  MesiCopter</a>, 1999, research project, external electronics, 1.5 cm rotor diameter

<li><a href="http://www.smartflyer.de/">
  Smartflyer</a>, 2003-2009, Complete family of really small quadrocopters, from 15g upwards

<li><a href="http://www.pascal.zunino.fr/microX4.htm">
  MicroX4</a>, 2006, 16 cm, 40g, CFK props, rotors 8 cm

<li><a href="http://members.vol.at/pi/">
  YAUP</a>, 2007, (Yet another UFO project),  X-Twin props

<li><a href="http://forum.mikrokopter.de/topic-13092.html">
  Nanocopter</a> X-Twin, integrated on one PCB
</ul>
